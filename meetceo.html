Meet the CEO

Dr. Victor Yamo embodies a lifelong dedication to animal welfare that spans over three decades of rich and diverse experience. With a foundation in veterinary surgery and poultry expertise honed at the University of Nairobi, his journey has taken him through various sectors, from corporate entities to governmental agencies and charitable organizations. Throughout his distinguished career, Dr. Yamo's passion for animal welfare has been unwavering. His work extends beyond the confines of traditional veterinary practice, delving into the intricate interplay between animal welfare, environmental sustainability, public health, and food safety. He continues to be at the forefront of initiatives aimed at enhancing the well-being of farm animals while simultaneously safeguarding the environment and promoting food security and livelihoods for producers.

Global One Health Advocacy Affairs is a non-governmental organization which is guided by the core values of care, compassion, fairness, advocacy and integrity in all aspects of our work.
